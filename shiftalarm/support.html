常見問題

通知沒有出現怎麼辦？

請先確認 iOS 系統設定中已允許 ShiftAlarm 傳送通知，並確認 App 內的備援通知功能已開啟。

行事曆匯出失敗怎麼辦？

請確認你已授予 ShiftAlarm 行事曆完整存取權限，並重新嘗試匯出。

iCloud 同步沒有立即更新怎麼辦？

iCloud 同步由系統管理，可能需要一些時間。請確認裝置已登入 iCloud，且網路連線正常。

購買後沒有解鎖怎麼辦？

請在 App 內使用「恢復購買」功能。如果仍無法解鎖，請透過支援信箱聯絡我們。

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I do if notifications do not appear?

Please check that notifications are allowed for ShiftAlarm in iOS Settings, and make sure backup notifications are enabled in the app.

What should I do if calendar export fails?

Please confirm that ShiftAlarm has full calendar access permission, then try exporting again.

What should I do if iCloud sync does not update immediately?

iCloud sync is managed by the system and may take some time. Please make sure your device is signed in to iCloud and has a working network connection.

What should I do if my purchase is not unlocked?

Please use the “Restore Purchases” feature in the app. If the issue continues, contact us by email.
