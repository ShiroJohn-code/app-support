Common Questions

Why are rates different from a bank or exchange counter?
Exchange rates are for reference only. Actual transaction rates, fees, and timing can differ by institution.

Can I use the app without location access?
Yes. Location is optional and only used when you choose to save a purchase place for a favorite.

How do I restore a support purchase?
Open the unlock screen in the app and tap Restore Purchases.

Report an Ad Issue

If a rewarded ad is inappropriate, broken, or does not grant the reward after completion, email shirojohncode@gmail.com with the approximate time, your region, and a short description.
