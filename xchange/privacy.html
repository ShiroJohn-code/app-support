Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 6, 2026

HelloM4 ("the app") is designed to keep your travel currency records on your device unless a feature explicitly uses a third-party service described below.

Data You Create

The app can store favorites, currency amounts, exchange-rate snapshots, optional titles, optional store names, optional photos, and optional location information. Favorites and photos are stored locally on your device.

Location

The app requests location access only when you tap the location button for a favorite. The location is used to save the purchase place and to reverse geocode an address. You can deny location access and continue using the app.

Photos and Camera

The app uses the camera only when you choose to take a photo for a favorite. Photo Library selection uses Apple's photo picker so you can choose specific images.

Exchange Rate Data

The app fetches exchange rates from Bank of Taiwan and may use open.er-api.com as a fallback source. Requests are used to provide app functionality and do not require an account.

Advertising

The app uses Google Mobile Ads to provide rewarded ads. The first App Store version is intended to request non-personalized rewarded ads. Google may process device, diagnostics, product interaction, advertising, and coarse location data according to Google's policies and the consent options available in your region.

Purchases

The app uses Apple's StoreKit for non-consumable support purchases. Apple processes purchase transactions. The app checks purchase entitlement locally through StoreKit.

Storage and Retention

Favorites, settings, cached rates, photos, and rewarded-ad unlock state are stored on your device using local storage. You can delete favorites in the app. Removing the app deletes its local data from the device according to iOS behavior.
